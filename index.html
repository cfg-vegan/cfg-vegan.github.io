About Us

We are two university students passionate about healthy eating and the saving our environment. We wanted to create a community to encourage people to adopt a vegan lifestyle and to give them the resources to make that change TODAY!


Why go Vegan


For the Planet

The production of meat and other animal products places a heavy burden on the environment. One of the most effective things an individual can do to lower their carbon footprint is to avoid all animal products.

For others

Avoiding animal products is the simplest way to take a stand against inefficient food systems which disproportionately affect the poorest people all over the world. Also it aids in reducing the rampant exploitation of animals

For yourself

Research has linked vegan diets with lower blood pressure and cholesterol, and lower rates of heart disease, type 2 diabetes and some types of cancer. Going vegan is a great opportunity to learn more about nutrition and cooking, and improve your diet.
